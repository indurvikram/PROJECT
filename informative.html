EDUREKA

Edureka is an online education and e-learning platform, privately owned and operated by Brain4ce Education Pvt. Ltd. headquartered in Bangalore. It provides courses in trending technologies like Big data, Cloud computing, blockchain and artificial intelligence. The platform offers live interactive sessions, masters programs and post graduate programs.

HISTORY

Edureka was founded in 2011 by Lovleen Bhatia and Kapil Tyagi. In 2005, the duo had together founded DByDx Software, a mobile app development company. In 2011 they sold DByDx Software to Kellton Tech for $3 million. Proceeds from this sale helped them fund Edureka with an initial self-investment of Rs. 1.5 crores. Vineet Chaturvedi joined as the third co-founder later
In June 2018, Edureka raised $2 million in the first round of funding from Venture firm Leo Capital India. With this investment, Rajul Garg, founder & managing partner of Leo Capital, joined the board of Edureka
In April 2019, NIT Warangal announced a Post Graduate Program in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in partnership with Edureka.
In June 2019, NIT Rourkela also partnered with Edureka to provide a Post Graduate Program in Big data engineering.
In August 2019, IMT Ghaziabad partnered with Edureka to offer live online courses in business management domain for executive certification courses.
In 2019, IIT Kanpur started a 9-month program for certification in cloud computing in collaboration with Edureka.

ABOUT US

At Edureka Learning Center, we believe in imparting the latest, in-demand, tech skills through our exclusively curated courses to bridge the gap between industry demand and talent supply.
Our mission is to provide you with an excellent learning experience and help you attract the jobs of your dreams. We provide learners with world-class content in an easy-to-use format and help them match the crazy pace of technological evolution. At Edureka Learning Center, our students are not mere learners, they are the future IT professionals.

Edureka Learning Center is powered by Edureka, the industry leader in the online professional and higher education sector in the emerging tech and management domains. Founded in 2011, Edureka pioneered Instructor-led live online training in India under the supervision of professional instructors, all under the umbrella motive of helping students upskill.
In September 2021, Veranda, a Public Listed entity, acquired Edureka as part of its mission to build a full-stack education business catering to different strata of learners encompassing virtually every level of Indian society.

Edureka Learning Center is part of Veranda Learning Solutions (VLS) Ltd. promoted by the Kalpathi AGS group; an enterprise headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The Group is well-known for providing students and young talent with industry-relevant learning and skills along with platforms to realize their employment potential.
